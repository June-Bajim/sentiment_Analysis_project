About Page

Senti is dedicated to providing timely and accurate prediction of sentiment polarity of products and give recommendations about the products based on the percentage of the sentiment polarity given to a product using the customer reviews. Our goal is to offer detailed prediction and recommendations that enable efficient decision-making to customers and producers.
